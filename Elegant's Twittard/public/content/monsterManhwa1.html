4 Monsters of Manhwa | Part 1

Manhwa, or what people used to call webtoon, is one of the misunderstood-est things (at least for me lol). Back then, I thought webtoon is just a bunch of romance or drama comics that I won't possibly touch, ever. But now, I open it, like, almost every day lol.

Ehem, so back to the topic, after reading 80+ titles, I found 4 manhwas that really stand out above the rest for some particular reasons, and what I mean by 'the rest' is the limit of what comics capable of itself. And those comics are :

1. Lookism

Let's start with one of the most famous webtoons in Indonesia, I really skeptical about this webtoon, u know, cus Indonesian peoples have sh*tty taste lol. The proof is, some of the best manhwas are at bottom of the list in the Indonesian version of Webtoon, but in the English version, those manhwas always staying at the top. What a loser. Umm, I'm just, gonna stop my ranting, this is not what I'm signed for.

Ok, so At first, I thought the story is about an ugly boy who suddenly transforms into a handsome man, and he gains a lot of popularity from girls or other boys (in a bad way). So we will see how he manages to hide the truth that he's actually an ugly and weak boy in everyday life, and at the end of the story, he will find someone who truly likes him as the way he is. The end. You know, like a classic romance story.

But no, that's completely wrong , turns out, this webtoon is all about fighting. Frankly speaking, this webtoon is the most all-around manhwa I've ever read. It has a funny arc, sad arc, scary arc, etc., and an almost non-existence romance arc, fortunately. It's kinda funny to think that it's 180 degrees different from what I've thought before. I'm not gonna spoil anything here, but believe me, the percentage is like this 20% Fighting scene, 50% drama that surrounding those fighting scenes, and the rest is just an ordinary drama (which eventually evolves around fighting too lmao).

That's it? is that the reason why I call it a monster? just a bunch of 'fighting' words and I lose my mind?

Yes

Ofc not, don't be silly. Now let's talk about the fun part. Say, what's the boring or weakest part of comic format compared to video? Exactly, it's the static image. Now, how can u overcome this? how can a mere bunch of static pictures surpass the glorified animated video? Tbh, I don't know what the list of the actual answer is, but one thing I notice after reading this comic is u can do that by putting f*cking plot twist on every goddamn panel, literally.

When an animated video tries to make a plot twist, it needs a good transition, build up, and momentum to deliver it perfectly. But within these static images, u can just do it however and whenever u want, and it would still look flawlessly. Moreover, the webtoon format(vertical scroll with a big image in each panel) is just perfect to execute this kind of thing, compared to manga which 1 page consists of several frames, which obviously not suitable for a twist, you will immediately get spoilered just by reading the top frames. The author of these comics seems to know this trick and he really utilizes this benefit to its maximum capacity.

You can or can't predict what r u gonna see on the next panel, but the end result would always be the same, you'll just gonna get shocked and impressed at the same time each time u scroll it.

It's just 1 of a particular set of skills that this comic has, but that's what I really admire, so I didn't include many other godly perks he has. And therefore, that's why I call this manhwa, a Monster.

Previously I mentioned How to Fight and Loser Life, go check it out, it has the same author and the same writing style too. It's almost as good as Lookism, actually, to put it simply, it just Lookism with a smaller scale. Oh, and they share the same universe too.

(Note: what I mean by plot twist doesn't necessarily mean like a big plot twist at the end of a movie, but sometimes it just a minor plot twist in a battle scene or just a twist moment in the drama scene, u should get my point. Oh, and if u think I'm exaggerating I dare u just give me 1 episode which doesn't have a twisting moment at all and I'll commit sudoku)

Final score: plot twist out of 10.

lmao, I didn't expect that it's gonna be this much just for talking about 1 manhwa. So, I'm just gonna call it a day for now and continue the second part later. (spoiler: it's gonna be a lot crazier than this, there's 1 manhwa that makes me wanna talk about this 'Monster' topic in the first place so..) Oh, and one last thing I wanna mention, in case u didn't know, u can read these manhwas on Line Webtoon for free, ok, see you later, bye.
